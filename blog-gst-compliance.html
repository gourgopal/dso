GST Compliance Made Easy with WeP Printers

With changing tax regulations, ensuring your business is GST compliant is crucial to avoid penalties. Thankfully, modern billing printers make this process seamless.

In-built GST Calculation

Standalone billing machines from WeP, such as the BP Joy and BP 2100 series, come with built-in features to configure CGST and SGST slabs. You simply select the item, and the machine automatically calculates the correct tax and prints it clearly on the receipt.

Detailed Tax Reports

At the end of the day or month, you can generate comprehensive tax reports directly from the printer. These reports can be handed over to your accountant for easy GST filing, saving you hours of manual calculation.

Updating Tax Slabs

When government tax slabs change, you can easily update the item rates in your WeP printer via a simple USB connection to a computer, ensuring you are always compliant.
